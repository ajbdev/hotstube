FAQ

Will this get me banned by Warden?

This program records your HOTS game in real time and then analyzes the replay file that HOTS creates after the game is finished. It does not analyze game data in real time, nor does it access or modify memory or game data files. To my knowledge, this means that Warden will not ban you for using this program. There are other HOTS related programs that operate similarly that have not resulted in Warden bannings (to my knowledge).

Okay, what does this thing do?

This program creates 10-20 second clips of your kills during a game. It does other things, too, such as showing a basic score screen and indexing all your games to make them searchable by map, player, or hero. There's a lot more planned down the road.

How can I help?

Run the program and play some HotS. As you run into bugs (and you probably will) send them to me (reddit /u/hophacker or discord). If you have any ideas/suggestions/feedback, small or large, please send them to me!

Will this slow down my computer while I play?

This software records your game as you play, therefore, yes - it will slow down your computer as much as any streaming/video recording software would. You can set the quality of your videos in the setting. The lower the quality, the less it will slow down your machine.

I can't see any highlights for games I've played

Make sure that A) you started HotSTube before the game started B) you are playing in windowed, or full screen windowed mode and C) You do not close or restart HotSTube mid-game. If these solutions do not fix your problem, please contact me to investigate.
